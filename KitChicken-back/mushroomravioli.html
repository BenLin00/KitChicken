Mushroom Ravioli

Prep Time: 1 hr | Cook Time: 10 min | Servings: 2
Ingredients
  • 1¼ cups all purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large porcini mushroom
  • 1/2 small onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 tablespoon mascarpone
  • 1½ tablespoon freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • oregano, parsley


Steps
  1. For filling, in a medium pan add butter, chopped mushrooms, chopped onion and garlic, cook until golden stirring occasionally. Then add oregano, parsley and cook 20 seconds.
  2. Remove 3 /4 of the mixture and blend until creamy. Add mascarpone and parmesan, stir to combine.
  3. For pasta, on a flat surface place four, make a well in the middle and add the eggs, with your fingers or a fork slowly mix together the flour and eggs to form a smooth dough. Cover and let rest one hour.
  4. Cut dough into 3-4 parts and roll out.
  5. Cut dough into even, 2x2 inch squares. Then fill with approximately 1 heaping teaspoon full of mushroom filling. Place another square on top and close the sides with the pastry crimper.
  6. In salted boiling water cook the ravioli for approximately 5 minutes.
  7. For sauce, in a large pan melt the butter and add the parsley, simmer for 1 minute. Add ravioli and a couple of tablespoons of pasta water, and toss to cover and sauté 30 seconds.
